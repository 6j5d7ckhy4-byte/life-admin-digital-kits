# Life Admin Digital Kits: 30-Day Operating Plan

## Working thesis

Build a low-risk micro-publishing and digital product project around practical life organization tools. Start with free helpful resources, then test paid bundles once there is enough useful material.

## Risk boundaries

- No professional legal, tax, medical, or financial advice.
- No collecting sensitive personal information in public forms.
- No paid ads until there is a clear reason.
- No inventory, shipping, or customer-service-heavy offers at the start.
- No external outreach or sales emails without Steph's review unless a narrow campaign is explicitly approved.

## Target customer

Busy adults who feel behind on paperwork, bills, documents, appointments, subscriptions, family responsibilities, and household admin.

## First offer ladder

1. Free: The 30-Minute Life Admin Reset
2. Low-cost: Life Admin Starter Kit
3. Bundle: Life Admin Binder Pack
4. Expansion packs:
   - Tax Prep Organizer
   - Family History Interview Kit
   - Home Maintenance Checklist
   - Medical Appointment Prep Sheet
   - Budget and Bill Reset Kit

## Week 1: Foundation

Deliverables:

- Public GitHub repository
- Landing page
- Free 30-minute reset asset
- 10 product/page ideas
- Operating plan

Success measure: a clear public project with one useful free asset.

## Week 2: First product draft

Create the Life Admin Starter Kit v1:

- Monthly Life Admin Checklist
- Bill and Subscription Tracker
- Tax Document Organizer
- Important Documents Inventory
- Appointment Prep Sheet
- Household Project Tracker
- Family Follow-Up Planner
- Weekly Reset Page
- Professional Questions Worksheet
- Safe Password Inventory Guidance Sheet

Success measure: a reviewable first bundle draft.

## Week 3: Landing and distribution

Create:

- Product landing page
- Gumroad/Payhip/Etsy comparison
- Product description
- Free download copy
- 5 short promotional posts
- FAQ and disclaimer

Success measure: Steph can decide whether to publish free, sell, or keep iterating.

## Week 4: Monetization review

Create a report with:

- What exists
- What can be sold
- Suggested price
- Required setup steps
- Risks and disclaimers
- Next 5 products to build

Success measure: choose continue, pivot, or pause.
