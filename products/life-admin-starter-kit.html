Life Admin Starter Kit

A simple bundle for getting bills, documents, appointments, subscriptions, household tasks, and family follow-ups out of your head and into one practical system.

Open the starter kit draft Back to home

Built for people who are busy, not people who love paperwork.

The kit is designed to be used in short sessions. Pick one area, write down what matters, choose three next actions, and move on.

What is included

Monthly reset

Checklist for bills, documents, appointments, household tasks, and follow-ups.

Money admin

Bill and subscription tracker to catch renewals, old cards, and forgotten charges.

Documents

Tax document organizer and important documents inventory with privacy reminders.

Appointments

Prep sheet for medical, dental, school, vet, home repair, or professional appointments.

Household

Project tracker that separates now, next, later, and drop.

Family follow-ups

A simple table for who needs what, by when, and how to follow up.
